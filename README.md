# 🎓 Farewell Tribute — Class of 2025, Senior High

> *A heartfelt digital farewell from the students of the 2025 batch to the teachers who shaped them.*

---

## 📌 About the Project

This is a **warm farewell tribute website** created by the students of the **2025 Senior High batch** for their teachers. The site allows each teacher to enter their name and be taken to a **personalized tribute page** — filled with heartfelt messages, memories, and gratitude from their students.

This is a **Beta / Work-in-Progress** version of the project. The core concept and template are in place; individual teacher pages are still being built out.

---

## ✨ How It Works

1. A teacher visits the site and is greeted by the **main landing page**.
2. They **type their name** into a search/input field.
3. Their name appears as a suggestion — they **click on it**.
4. They are taken to their own **personal tribute page**, unique to them.
5. The page displays **messages from students** of the 2025 batch, written especially for that teacher.

## 🛠️ How to Add a New Teacher Page

1. Duplicate `template.html` and rename it (e.g., `ms-sharma.html`).
2. Fill in the teacher's **name**, **subject**, and **photo**.
3. Add **student messages** in the designated message section.
4. Register the teacher's name in the main page's name list so the search can find and link to their page.
